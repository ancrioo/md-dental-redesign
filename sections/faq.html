FAQ

Answers that make the first visit easier.

What happens during a cosmetic consultation?

We discuss your goals, review photos or scans when needed, and outline treatment routes with timing and comfort considerations.

Can treatment be staged over multiple visits?

Yes. Many smile plans are phased so health, budget, healing, and aesthetic milestones stay manageable.

Do you offer care for nervous patients?

We prioritize slower pacing, clear explanations, comfort breaks, and a visit plan that reduces surprises.

How do I know which service to book?

Choose a consultation when you are unsure. The team can route urgent pain, preventive checkups, and cosmetic goals appropriately.
