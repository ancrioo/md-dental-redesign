Doctor profile

Meet the clinicians behind MD Dental.

Periodontal surgeon and oral implantologist

Dr. Faizan Sharif

B.D.S, M.D.S

Dr. Faizan Sharif started MD Dental in 2018 with a vision to serve high quality advanced dentistry at affordable prices. Full mouth dental implant rehabilitation is his forte, with work in lasers, immediate implant loading, bone grafting, soft tissue grafting and gummy smile treatment.

Cosmetic dentist and oral implantologist

Dr. Anam F Sharif

B.D.S, M.I.D.A
